About Me

I am currently pursuing my Master's in Signal Processing at IISc Bangalore under the guidance of Associate Professor Prasanta Kumar Ghosh. My research focus involves developing a system for converting whispered speech into normal speech.

Projects

Two Stage Transformer Based approach for variable length abstractive text summarization

Implemented a two-stage method for variable-length abstractive summarization, integrating BERT-based text segmentation with a Transformer-based summarization module. Achieved 70.0% accuracy in human subjective evaluation on the LCSTS dataset.

Speech Emotion Recognition using a DualStream CNN-Transformer Fusion Network

Engineered a Dual-Stream CNN-Transformer Fusion Network to accurately analyze and classify speech-based emotional states. The approach effectively combined convolutional and transformer-based architectures to capture both local and global features, enabling improved performance in emotion recognition tasks.

INTRUSION DETECTION SYSTEM

Designed, implemented, and fine-tuned deep learning models to detect anomalous patterns and classify potential threats within network traffic which improved the IDS's accuracy by 10% through adversarial (GANs) models.
